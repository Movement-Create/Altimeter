About the Tesseract

A tesseract is the 4D analog of a cube. Just as a cube consists of 6 square faces, a tesseract consists of 8 cubical cells.

Drag to rotate in 3D, use sliders to rotate in 4D dimensions (X-W, Y-W, Z-W).

Enable auto-rotation for a beautiful continuous animation!
